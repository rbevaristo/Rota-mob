ROUTA - Services STAFF SCHEDULING The app produces a schedule for your employees based on your demands. Freely adjust the factors that you want to affect the generation of your staffs’ schedule. PERFORMANCE EVALUATION Managers can continuously record their employees’ performance in the app. It allows them to see the progress of their staff’s performance over time which can aid in their professional decision making. REPORT GENERATION Data regarding the employees’ schedule, and evaluated performance, easily accessible by the manager through the use of the app.
